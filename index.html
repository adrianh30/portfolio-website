SHPE National Convention

During the first semester, I actively engaged with my organization from the very beginning, which led to a remarkable opportunity. Among the freshman class, I was one of three members chosen to attend the SHPE National Convention. This convention was an amazing experience, providing a multitude of events and workshops that significantly contributed to my professional growth and development. I had the opportunity to compete in the cybersecurity competition with four of my colleagues and then proceed to attend the career fair, where I networked with professionals and other students from around the country.

TAMU SHPE Internal Affairs

In the SHPE chapter at Texas A&M, we have different committees lead by officers that carry out different tasks and respobsibilities for our members. I am actively involved in the Internal Affairs committee where we organize, set up, and carry out social, volunteering and athletic events.

SHPE Athletics

In the SHPE Internal Affairs Comittee, athletic events are something big that brings everyone together.
